Location and Time

Piscataway Codes will be hosted in Piscataway High School (100 Behmer Road, Piscataway NJ, 08854) on May 20th from 8:45AM to 3:00PM.

About the Event

Students will learn how to use Scratch as well as other programing concepts and will work on projects in groups, while being supervised by our mentors. Breakfeast and Lunch will be served free of charge and students will be provided a t-shirt and access to a computer, along with other possible prizes.

group

Requirements

We would like childeren of grades 4 to 8 from Piscataway to participate. This is an introduction to computer science, so no background knowledge is required.

A Wonderful First Step in Computer Science for Childeren.

More Information

This is a 100% free event designed to expose students to code and similar STEM concepts at a young age. The aim of Piscataway Codes is to allow all members of our community to be literate in understanding code and to prepare students with the skills they will need later on in life. Piscataway Codes is run by a dedicated team of students and teachers who have a passion for computer science and who want to help the community, without gain. Just as Piscataway Reads is on a mission to spread literature among our youth and promote language arts, so too is Piscataway Codes on a mission to spread computer science throughout our community and promote Science Technology Engineering and Math among our childeren.

Completely Free of Charge; Food and Technology Provided.
